Meet the Founder

Kumar Raushan

The Visionary Behind Rani Pharma

With over 15 years of experience in pharmaceutical distribution, Kumar Raushan established Rani Pharma with a simple mission: to make quality medicines accessible and affordable for every pharmacy and hospital in Bihar.

Starting from a small shop in Alalpatti, Darbhanga, the business has grown into one of the most trusted wholesale medicine suppliers in the region. His commitment to quality, fair pricing, and exceptional customer service has earned the trust of over 500 pharmacies and healthcare institutions.
